GTM Messaging Analysis: Sample Call 01

Customer Themes

- Access management is fragmented across environments  
- Processes for access are manual and inconsistent  
- Buyers are concerned about adding complexity  
- Buyers need concrete, day-to-day examples  

Objections

- Perceived overlap with existing IAM tools  
- Concern about integration with legacy systems  

Customer Language

- “It’s not centralized in any meaningful way” → signals fragmentation  
- “We don’t want to introduce more complexity” → resistance to layered solutions  

Messaging Gaps

- Unclear how the platform differs from IAM  
- Lack of concrete workflow examples  

Positioning Recommendations

- Emphasize unification rather than adding another layer  
- Show clear, practical before-and-after workflows  

Field Enablement Notes

- Focus demos on daily engineer workflows  
- Prepare integration examples for legacy environments
